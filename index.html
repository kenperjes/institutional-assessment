Why is coffee the world's beloved beverage?

Here we will show you the numerous benefits and nutrients that you will get from drinking coffee.
Benefits

Coffee boost alertness, enhance physical performance, improve mood, provide rich antioxidants, enhance cognitive function, and more.

Nutrients

Coffee contains a number of useful nutrients, including riboflavin (vitamin B2), niacin (vitamin B3), magnesium, potassium, and various phenolic compounds, or antioxidants.


Most Popular Kinds of Coffee:

Espresso

It is characterized by its strong flavor and rich texture, often served in small shots called "singles" or "doubles". Espresso serves as the base for many other coffee drinks like cappuccinos, lattes, and macchiatos.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is a popular Italian coffee drink made with espresso, hot milk, and steamed milk foam. It typically has equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, resulting in creamy texture with light, airy foam on top.

Latte

A latte is a coffe drink made with espresso and steamed milk. It has more foam, higher ratio of steamed milk to espresso, resulting in a smoother and creamier texture. Lattes are often topped with a thin layer of milk foam and can be customised with flavor syrups.

Top 3 Best-selling Coffee Brews

Here are the three of the best-selling coffee brews from coffee shops worldwide with its short description each.
Drip-Coffee

Drip coffee: it's made by pouring hot water over ground coffee beans contained in a filter, allowing the water to drip through and extract flavors. It's simple and accessible.

Cold Brew

Cold Brew: It's made by steeping coarse coffee grounds in cold water for an extended period, typically 12-24 hours, which results in smooth, mellow brew with lower acidity compared to traditional hot-brewed coffee.

Cafe Americano

Cafe Americano: a simple yet satisfying coffee drink by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, resulting in a beverage similar in strength and flavor to drip coffee. Popular for its bold and versatility.
